About Us

Freaky Productionz is a South African streetwear label with roots in the underground and rebellious youth street culture that dominates Mzanzi's urban centers. Otlotleng Rammekwa, the brand's founder and head designer, is the inspiration behind Freaky Productionz. In 2020, he identified a market need for a brand created by and for young people. Freaky Productionz was founded on ambition, perseverance, and teamwork. The Freaky Productionz aesthetic is distinguished by its innovative designs, which are realized through a 21st-century African design philosophy that reflects the modern aspirations of South African youth. The brand is inspired by South Africa's "new age" youth, who constantly redefine what it means to be African, young, wild, and free.
